About Me

Currently pursuing a career in web development, to achieve my goal I have taken an extensive course where I have learned to operate with the latest technologies to create, manage, and deploy modern websites and web applications. I have gained enough knowledge to operate with HTML, CSS, javascript, jquery, node.js, MySQL, MongoDB, and I have developed a deep understanding of back-end programming, during my studies I improved the ability to think logically in a form that allows me to create and turn that creativity into a program that the computer can process and display back an interface, fully dynamic and with complete back-end properties.

From the Caribbean, lover of Afro-descendant rhythms, music is truly a passion for me and a way to find peace and joy, creating and innovating has always been my interest, I believe that came from my father since he was very interested in all kind of new computerized technologies, and worked for the I. T department of a very important oil company "P.D.V.S. A" he was also a musician and a singer, so I truly appreciate inheriting that from him

The state of Texas, more specifically in the city of Houston is where I attended Middle School and High School and it was there where I learned English, in Middle School I took a " web mastering" class, and from that moment I became fascinated with the things I could do on a computer and then be able to display it on the world-wide-web. I participated in the making of the school website and I felt really proud of being able to be part of that.

I am a sports fanatic, basketball and surfing are my main two sports that I practice. Exploring other countries and knowing other cultures is also my interest, besides the U.S. and Venezuela, I also lived in England, Australia, and France. Now that I am a bit more financially stable I have decided to pursue one of my passions which is to become a full-stack web developer, something that caught my attention from a young age.
